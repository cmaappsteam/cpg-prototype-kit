Report businesses agreeing not to compete with each other

A business cartel occurs when 2 or more businesses agree that they wwill not compete with each other>

This may include:

  • Fixing prices
  • Agreeing not to compete for jobs (bid-rigging)
  • Sharing markets (for example, by geographical area)
  • Sharing sensitive operational information between businesses

You can report cartel information to us using the dedicated online reporting form

You may be eligible for a financial reward if you give us information that leads to an investigation, for more information read Cartels: policy for witnessing and reporting.

Apply for leniency
